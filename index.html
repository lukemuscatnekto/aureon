Ask your coach anything. Get real answers.

The AI coach knows everything — your training history, your nutrition, your recovery. Ask it why you're not progressing, what to eat before a heavy squat day, or whether you're overtraining. It tells you exactly what it sees in your data.

Why haven't my squat numbers moved in 3 weeks?
Looking at your last 21 days — your weekly volume has been consistent, but your average sleep is 5.8 hours and your Tuesday sessions (your heaviest squat day) come after your most demanding Monday cardio. Your CNS isn't recovered. Try pushing squats to Wednesday and watch the numbers move.
